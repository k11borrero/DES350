a. My interpretation is that design can evolve and change for the better. What works today may not work as well in the future and design should be able to adapt and change.


b. Light Bulb.


c. In the future I would make sure my products have room to grow, I would constantly be experimenting with new ideas.

b. Slinky.


c. As I continue with design I will work to not forget that apart from designing something I have to make sure it is useful. the product is of no use if it doesn't work how it is suppose to.

a. The product needs to be appealing to the user, it needs to fit in the users lifestyle and work correctly.


b. Instant Pot.


c. I have never really thought about this concept before. In the future I will try and take this into account when creating new things.

4. Good design makes a product understandable.

a. No caption or description is needed for good design. It can stand on its own. The product is able to speak for itself and express its function.


b. Silicone baking mat.


c. In the future I will try to create designs that can be understood without having to be described. If the description of the product is lost it should still be useful, and understood.
